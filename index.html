Our History

Blanco River Pizza was founded with a simple mission: to bring authentic and delicious pizza to our community. Established in 1995, we started as a small, family-owned restaurant, dedicated to using fresh, locally sourced ingredients and traditional recipes. Over the years, our passion for quality and customer satisfaction has fueled our growth, turning us into a beloved local favorite. From our classic Margherita to our innovative gourmet selections, we aim to create unforgettable dining experiences. Today, Blanco River Pizza continues to expand while maintaining the core values of great taste and community spirit that have always defined us.
